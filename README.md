## Overview

The **DMS Leadership Accelerator** is a high-touch, evidence-based leadership development program designed for mid-to-senior leaders operating in complex, distributed, and matrixed organizations. 

Participants develop advanced capabilities in systems thinking, cross-functional influence, adaptive decision-making, and team orchestration—skills proven to drive measurable business outcomes in today’s hybrid and global environments.

**Target Audience**:  
- Directors, Senior Managers, and High-Potential Leaders  
- Leaders managing distributed, remote, or matrixed teams  
- High-performers preparing for enterprise-level leadership roles

**Cohort Size**: 12–18 participants per cohort (for optimal peer learning and coaching depth)

## Program Objectives

By the end of the 12-week Accelerator, participants will be able to:

- Apply systems leadership principles to diagnose and resolve complex organizational challenges
- Build and sustain high-trust, high-performance teams across geographic and functional boundaries
- Leverage influence and negotiation strategies without direct authority
- Drive strategic alignment and execution in ambiguous, fast-changing environments
- Demonstrate measurable improvements in leadership effectiveness (measured via 360° feedback and business KPIs)

## Key Outcomes & Business Impact

- **Expected ROI**: 3–5x within 12 months (based on historical cohort data)
- Average improvement in leadership competency scores: **+42%** (post-program 360° assessment)
- Documented increase in team engagement scores and cross-functional project velocity

## Program Structure

**Total Duration**: 12 weeks  
**Learning Modality**: Blended (synchronous + asynchronous + applied practice)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–3)
- Systems Thinking & Organizational Diagnosis
- Self-Awareness & Leadership Presence in Virtual/Hybrid Settings
- Building Psychological Safety at Scale

### Phase 2: Core Capabilities (Weeks 4–8)
- Influence Without Authority
- Matrix Leadership & Stakeholder Orchestration
- Adaptive Decision-Making & Scenario Planning
- Coaching & Developing Distributed Teams

### Phase 3: Application & Integration (Weeks 9–12)
- Strategic Initiative Design & Execution
- Capstone Leadership Challenge (real business project)
- Peer Coaching Circles & Executive Feedback Sessions
- Personal Leadership Legacy Plan

**Weekly Commitment**: 4–6 hours (including live sessions, self-paced modules, and applied assignments)

## Learning Components

- **Live Virtual Masterclasses** (facilitated by senior DMS faculty + guest executives)
